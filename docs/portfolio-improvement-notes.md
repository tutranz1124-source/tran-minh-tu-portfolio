# Ghi Chu Cai Thien Portfolio Va Background

Ngay luu: 2026-03-10
Muc dich: tai lieu hoa cac huong cai thien ve hero, experience, skills, typography, CTA, responsive va background de dung lam checklist cho cac lan polish tiep theo.

## I. Cac Diem Can Sua Chi Tiet

### 1. Hero Section

#### 1.1. Van de hien tai
- Hero da dep hon nhieu so voi cac ban dau, nhung van hoi hien va hoi day chu.
- Ten da noi bat, nhung pitch chinh va cac doan mo ta phia duoi van hoi sat nhau, chua tao duoc cam giac "thoang" va "cao cap".
- Card thong tin ben phai da can bo cuc hon, nhung van hoi giong mot khoi support de lap cho trong, chua that su la mot diem neo thi giac manh.
- 2 nut CTA hien on nhung chua co su phan cap rat ro giua nut chinh va nut phu.

#### 1.2. Cach cai thien
- Tach hero thanh 3 tang thong tin rat ro:
  - Tang 1: Ten, lon nhat, dam nhat, sang nhat.
  - Tang 2: Cau pitch chinh, phai la thong diep dinh vi manh nhat sau ten.
  - Tang 3: Cac doan mo ta phu, nhe hon, mo hon, line-height rong hon.
- Tang khoang trang giua ten, subtitle, pitch va body text.
- Giam do sang cua body text khoang 10-15% so voi pitch de tranh canh tranh thi giac.
- Lam nut chinh noi hon nua bang glow mem hon, contrast ro hon hoac width nhinh hon mot chut.
- Nut phu nen nhe hon ro rang de nguoi xem hieu ngay CTA nao la hanh dong uu tien.

#### 1.3. Goi y cho card thong tin ben phai
- Bien no thanh "mini profile dashboard" thay vi box phu.
- Role hien tai phai la thanh phan noi bat nhat ben trong card.
- 3 khoi thong tin ben duoi (kinh nghiem, core stack, trong tam) nen co khoang cach ro hon.
- Label cua tung khoi nen nho hon, tracking rong hon de giong he thong UI thay vi dong text thuong.
- Co the them line accent rat nhe o canh tren hoac vi tri vien sang hon mot chut de card nay co suc nang rieng.

### 2. Section Kinh Nghiem

#### 2.1. Van de hien tai
- Day la section manh nhat hien tai, nhung doi luc no lai chiem qua nhieu suc nang thi giac so voi hero.
- Cot timeline ben trai van hoi ton ngang so voi gia tri thong tin mang lai.
- 3 card co cau truc qua giong nhau nen khi cuon xuong de co cam giac lap.
- Text trong card da ro hon truoc, nhung van hoi sit, nhat la cac phan "Muc tieu", "Tac dong/Ket qua", "Tech".
- Khu media ben trai card hien van co nguy co tro thanh khu trang tri neu khong to chuc lai khi thay bang logo tech/platform.

#### 2.2. Cach cai thien bo cuc
- Thu hep cot timeline ben trai mot chut de nhuong khong gian cho card noi dung.
- Giu timeline dep, nhung giam vai tro "khoi lon" cua no; no nen la truc dan huong, khong phai thanh phan tranh spotlight.
- Tao nhip giua 3 card:
  - Card cong viec hien tai la card noi bat nhat.
  - Card freelance co the gon hon, linh hoat hon.
  - Card con lai co the nhan vao impact hoac moi truong lam viec.
- Khong can doi layout lon, chi can khac nhau nhe ve hierarchy, do sang vien, muc do accent va cach nhan thong diep.

#### 2.3. Cach cai thien noi dung card
Moi card nen duoc doc theo thu tu nay:
- Vai tro
- Cong ty / du an
- Boi canh / loai du an
- Impact chinh
- Muc tieu
- Tech stack

Trong do:
- Impact sentence phai la cau manh nhat sau tieu de vai tro.
- Muc tieu la thong tin bo sung, khong nen nang bang impact.
- Tech stack chi la lop thong tin ho tro, khong nen chiem qua nhieu chu y.
- Label nhu "Muc tieu", "Tac dong", "Tech" nen nho hon, tracking rong hon, style giong label UI.
- Tang line-height cho body text trong card de do doc tot hon tren nen toi.

#### 2.4. Ve viec thay bieu tuong 3 vong tron bang logo tech/platform
Huong nay la dung, vi no bien phan minh hoa thanh phan co y nghia.

Tuy nhien can giu cac nguyen tac sau:
- Moi card chi nen dung 3-5 logo tieu bieu.
- Tat ca logo phai dong nhat ve kich thuoc, padding va cach dat.
- Khong nen de qua nhieu mau thuong hieu goc qua gat.
- Nen giam saturation hoac dua ve cung mot he hien thi de tranh vo tong the dark-tech.
- Nen dat logo trong mot "project stack panel" co bo cuc ro rang, khong nen la mot o den rong roi dat logo vao.
- Card nao it logo thi van phai co bo cuc dep, can doi, khong de panel ben trai trong phang.
- Co the them label nho nhu "Platform / Stack used" neu muon tang tinh doc hieu.

Muc tieu cua panel nay:
- Nhin vao la thay ngay du an dung cong nghe nao.
- Van giu duoc su sang, gon, co he thong.
- Khong bien section kinh nghiem thanh mot khu tap hop logo thuong hieu loe loet.

### 3. Section Ky Nang

#### 3.1. Van de hien tai
- Sach, gon, de scan, nhung hoi an toan.
- Cac box dang hoi ngang cap qua muc.
- Chua the hien ro ngay the manh chinh cua ban la backend, he thong va van hanh.

#### 3.2. Cach cai thien
- Uu tien thi giac cho cac nhom chu luc:
  - Backend
  - He thong
  - Van hanh
- Cac nhom nay co the sang hon chut, glow ro hon chut, hoac duoc dat o vi tri uu tien hon.
- Mo ta cua moi nhom nen co ca tinh ro hon, khong nen qua giong nhau ve nhip viet.
- Tang khoang cach giua title, mo ta va tag.
- Giam bot so tag neu thay qua day; uu tien tag tieu bieu nhat.
- Muc tieu la de section ky nang khong chi la danh sach cong nghe, ma la mot khu vuc "chot ha nang luc".

### 4. Typography

#### 4.1. Van de hien tai
- Font hop phong cach, nhung body text o mot so cho van nho va sit.
- Tren nen toi, text sat nhau rat de gay met khi doc.
- Cac cap do thong tin chua chenh du manh o mot vai khu vuc.

#### 4.2. Cach cai thien
- Tang line-height cho body text trong hero va card kinh nghiem.
- Giam do sang cua text phu de nhan text chinh.
- Tang khoang cach giua cac block text va label.
- Thong nhat hon cach viet hoa, tracking, do dam font cho label va heading.
- Tao contrast ro hon giua:
  - Heading
  - Subheading
  - Impact sentence
  - Body text
  - Labels

### 5. CTA Va Micro-Hierarchy

#### 5.1. Van de hien tai
- Nut CTA chinh va phu da on, nhung chua "keo mat" du tot.
- Mot so thanh phan phu van co do noi ngang ngang nhau, tao cam giac giao dien dep nhung chua that su co cao trao.

#### 5.2. Cach cai thien
- Nut CTA chinh nen la diem nhan ro o hero.
- Nut phu can im hon ro rang.
- Card hien tai / role hien tai nen co uu tien thi giac cao nhat.
- Muc tieu la moi section deu co mot diem "dung mat", khong phai thanh phan nao cung dep deu nhu nhau.

### 6. Responsive / Mobile

#### 6.1. Van de hien tai
- Mobile da kha on, khong vo bo cuc.
- Tuy nhien section kinh nghiem van dai va nhieu lop thong tin.
- Trai nghiem mobile hien nghieng ve doc ky hon la quet nhanh.

#### 6.2. Cach cai thien
- Tren mobile, impact sentence phai la thanh phan noi nhat trong moi card.
- Co the rut gon text phu, uu tien thong diep chinh.
- Timeline nen don gian hon o mobile, tranh chiem qua nhieu chieu dai.
- Panel media/logo ben trai co the gon hon o mobile de uu tien text.
- Muc tieu la de nguoi xem mobile chi can quet 3 lop:
  - Ban la ai
  - Ban dang lam gi
  - Ban tao ra gia tri gi

### 7. Floating Button / Chi Tiet Phu Noi

#### 7.1. Van de hien tai
- Nut tron noi ben phai tren mot so viewport hoi "ngoai he" so voi phan con lai cua ngon ngu thiet ke.
- De hut mat du no khong phai thanh phan chinh.

#### 7.2. Cach cai thien
- Neu giu lai: lam nho hon, chim hon, hoac chi hien ro khi hover.
- Neu khong qua quan trong: can nhac giam do hien dien hoac bo.
- Muc tieu la tranh phan tu phu tranh spotlight voi hero va card kinh nghiem.

## II. Huong Thiet Ke Background Moi

Muc tieu chung:
Background moi can tinh xao hon, sau hon va dep hon, nhung van phai giu noi dung la trung tam. Khong di theo huong "them nhieu hoa tiet", ma di theo huong "nen da lop, co anh sang co chu dich, co texture, co khong gian va co ngon ngu ky thuat rat nhe". Background tot nhat la nen nhin xa thi sach, nhin ky moi thay nhieu lop chi tiet.

### 1. Lop 1 - Nen Goc (Base Atmosphere)
- Dung mot gradient lon, chuyen mem giua navy, blue-black va mot chut cyan lanh.
- Khong de gradient qua ro; muc tieu la tao cam giac sau, khong phai to mau lo.
- Nung do toi nhat o ria ngoai va cac vung it thong tin.
- Vung sau hero va quanh timeline co the sang hon mot chut, nhung rat mem.

Cam giac can dat:
- Sau
- Lanh
- Hien dai
- Khong phang

### 2. Lop 2 - Glow Theo Section (Section-Driven Lighting)
Background nen phuc vu bo cuc, khong chi phu dep toan trang.

#### 2.1. Glow sau Hero
- Mot vung glow lon, mem, lan rong tu sau ten sang card ben phai.
- Muc tieu la lam hero tro thanh khu vuc co tam ly "mo bai" manh nhat.
- Glow nay nen la cyan rat nhe, blur sau, canh mem.

#### 2.2. Glow theo truc Kinh nghiem
- Mot dai sang dung, rat mo, rat nhe, chay doc theo timeline.
- Muc tieu la tang cam giac dan huong khi cuon.
- Khong de no giong den neon; phai rat mem, chi la mot "light tunnel" nhe.

#### 2.3. Glow sau section Ky nang
- Nhe hon hero.
- Co vai tro giu nhiet cho phan cuoi trang, nhung khong can tranh spotlight.

### 3. Lop 3 - Grain / Noise Sieu Min
- Phu mot lop grain/noise rat min len toan trang.
- Opacity rat thap, chi du de cam thay nen khong bi qua sach.
- Lop nay rat quan trong vi giao dien dark de bi "ky thuat so qua sach", nhin giong render phang.
- Grain lam background organic hon, premium hon, co chieu sau hon.

Luu y:
- Tuyet doi khong de grain qua manh.
- Nhin binh thuong gan nhu khong thay, chi thay nen "co chat" hon.

### 4. Lop 4 - Pattern Ky Thuat Rat Nhe
Co the them mot trong cac huong sau:
- Luoi mo (grid rat nhat)
- Duong cheo sieu min
- Contour line mo
- Node-line pattern rat nhe goi y ha tang he thong / flow du lieu

Cach dung:
- Dat chu yeu o ria ngoai trang va nhung vung it noi dung.
- Gan noi dung thi pattern phai mo dan hoac bien mat.
- Muc tieu la tao "dau van cong nghe", khong phai trang tri lo lieu.

Huong phu hop voi portfolio nay:
- Node, line, flow, grid nhe -> hop hon vi phan anh dung tinh chat backend / he thong / van hanh.

### 5. Lop 5 - Haze / Flare / Halo O Ria Man Hinh
- Them vai doi tuong anh sang mo o cac goc man hinh:
  - doi sang rat mo
  - bong halo blur nang
  - vet sang mem
  - khoi so hoa rat nhe
- Khong dat gan text.
- Chi dat o ria, sau section, hoac goc xa de tao cam giac khong gian.
- Co the giu 1 doi sang o goc tren phai, nhung can lam no an he hon vao he thong anh sang chung.

Muc tieu:
- Nen song hon
- Co khong khi hon
- Van giu su yen tinh

### 6. Lop 6 - Chieu Sau Khong Gian (Depth Layers)
Background dep hon khi co 3 tang cam giac:
- Hau canh: gradient toi, glow xa
- Trung canh: grid/pattern/noise
- Tien canh: haze blur hoac vet sang mo o mep man hinh

Hien tai background cua ban dep nhung van la mot "mat phang dep".
Can nang cap thanh "mot moi truong thi giac".

### 7. Quan He Giua Background Va Ngon Ngu Thiet Ke Cua Trang
Background nen noi dung hoi voi portfolio, khong nen chi la nen dark chung chung.

Voi ho so cua ban, background rat hop voi ngon ngu:
- infrastructure
- system flow
- data path
- node / signal / grid
- technical environment

Dieu nay phu hop hon cac nen my thuat chung chung, vi no phan anh ro ban la nguoi lam:
- backend
- he thong
- van hanh
- desktop app
- tich hop va giao vanh san pham

Tuc la background khong chi dep, ma con "dung nguoi".

### 8. Nguyen Tac Quan Trong Nhat Khi Lam Background Moi
- Khong them chi tiet bang contrast manh.
- Them bang su chenh lech nho ve sang toi, blur, texture va lop.
- Moi chi tiet nen di trong opacity thap, canh mem, blur cao, tong mau thong nhat.
- Background phai thi thang, khong duoc noi to.
- Noi dung luon la nhan vat chinh.

## III. De Xuat Mo Ta Background Moi Theo 1 Concept Hoan Chinh

Background moi nen duoc hinh dung nhu sau:

"Mot nen xanh den da lop, co gradient sau va lanh, duoc chia anh sang theo tung section. Hero co mot quang glow mem lon de mo bai manh hon. Section Kinh nghiem co mot truc sang doc rat nhe chay theo timeline de dan mat khi cuon. Toan bo trang duoc phu mot lop grain sieu min de nen co chat va premium hon. O ria ngoai la cac pattern ky thuat rat mo nhu grid, node-line hoac contour line goi y ha tang he thong. Tai cac goc co mot vai haze, halo va vet sang blur nang de tao cam giac khong gian, nhung khong duoc anh huong den vung doc. Tong the background phai giu dark-tech, hien dai, co chieu sau, tinh xao, nhin sach o lan dau nhung nhin ky se thay rat nhieu lop thi giac."

## IV. Thu Tu Uu Tien Neu Chi Sua Tung Buoc

Neu muon lam theo thu tu hieu qua nhat, nen uu tien:

1. Polish hero (khoang trang, hierarchy, CTA, card thong tin)
2. Thu hep va tinh timeline o section Kinh nghiem
3. Chuan hoa panel logo tech/platform cho tung du an
4. Tang hierarchy cho impact sentence trong moi card
5. Uu tien thi giac cho nhom ky nang chu luc
6. Lam lai background theo he thong da lop
7. Cuoi cung moi them micro-interaction / motion rat nhe
